Welcome to Boop Squad — not just a grooming service, but a full‑fledged coat conditioning experience. Our mission is to transform your dog’s coat from dull to dazzling, using only the finest botanical conditioners, essential oils, and pH‑balanced formulations. We believe that a healthy coat is a reflection of a happy pup, and we treat every dog like family.

Why coat conditioning matters

Your dog’s coat is more than just fur — it’s a protective barrier, a temperature regulator, and a key indicator of overall wellness. Regular conditioning replenishes natural oils, prevents breakage, reduces shedding, and keeps the skin hydrated. At Boop Squad, we go beyond the surface: our deep‑penetrating conditioners restore elasticity, add volume, and give that show‑ready gloss. Whether your pup has a thick double coat, curly poodle locks, or a short sleek layer, we tailor every treatment.

Our signature conditioning process

Every Boop Squad session starts with a gentle, sulfate‑free wash to open the cuticles. Then we apply our custom‑blended conditioning mask — infused with argan oil, shea butter, and oat extract — and let it sit for 5–7 minutes while giving a relaxing scalp massage. After a thorough rinse, we follow with a lightweight leave‑in conditioner and finish with a warm air fluff. The result? A coat that feels like silk, smells like heaven, and stays fresh for weeks.

What makes Boop Squad different?

We are not a typical grooming salon. We are a conditioning‑first squad. Our team of certified coat therapists spends 45–60 minutes per session, focusing solely on the health and beauty of the coat. We use cold‑pressed oils, organic aloe vera, and never any harsh sulfates or parabens. Plus, every session includes a complimentary paw balm and a spritz of our signature lavender‑chamomile mist.

We also believe in education: we teach you how to maintain that fresh‑from‑the‑squad glow between visits. From brushing techniques to the best home‑friendly conditioners, we empower you to be your pup’s first line of coat care.

Frequently asked questions

How often should I condition my dog? We recommend a deep conditioning every 4–6 weeks, with a light leave‑in in between. Over‑conditioning can weigh down the coat, so we guide you based on breed and texture.

Is Boop Squad suitable for puppies? Absolutely! We use ultra‑gentle, unscented formulas for puppies under 6 months. Their coats are delicate, and we take extra time to ensure a stress‑free experience.

Do you offer mobile services? Yes! We have a fully equipped mobile conditioning van that brings the spa to your doorstep. Perfect for anxious pups or busy pet parents.

Behind the scenes: our squad

Boop Squad was founded by two animal behaviorists and a cosmetic chemist who shared a vision: to elevate coat care to a holistic ritual. We train every team member in canine body language, so your dog feels safe and understood. Our conditioning stations are equipped with heated tables, aromatherapy diffusers, and calming music.

We also partner with local shelters — for every 10 conditioning sessions, we donate a full grooming kit to a rescue dog in need. Because every pup deserves to feel beautiful.

Our continuous innovation led to the creation of the “Boop Meter” — a coat health index that tracks moisture, elasticity, and shine over time. We share this report with you after each session, so you can see the progress. It’s not just a service; it’s a journey.

We’ve served over 480 dogs in the last two years, from Chihuahuas to Great Danes. Each coat has its own personality, and we celebrate that diversity. Whether it’s a double‑coated shepherd or a silky Yorkie, our techniques are adjusted down to the last detail.

We also host monthly “Coat Clinics” where we demonstrate advanced brushing and conditioning methods. These free workshops are open to all, and they’ve become a beloved community event.

Boop Squad is more than a business — it’s a movement towards mindful, loving coat care. We’re here to answer your questions, celebrate your pup’s uniqueness, and make every coat shine with pride.

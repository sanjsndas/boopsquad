Appendix: Detailed service policies

For added clarity, we provide the following detailed explanations of our key policies and procedures.

Matting & de‑matting policy

Matting occurs when dead fur tangles with healthy hair, forming tight knots. While we attempt to gently de‑mat using conditioners and specialised tools, severe matting can cause pain, skin irritation, and hidden infections. In such cases, we may need to shave the affected area (with your consent). Shaving is always a last resort and is performed with the dog’s comfort as the priority.

We charge an additional fee for extensive de‑matting, based on the time and effort required. We will inform you of any extra charges before proceeding.

Mobile service logistics

Our mobile conditioning van is fully equipped with water tanks, heating, and grooming stations. When you book a mobile appointment, please ensure:

  • There is clear, safe parking within 10 metres of your entrance.
  • Your dog is calm and accessible (we recommend a short walk before the session).
  • A designated area is available for us to set up (e.g., driveway, quiet street).
  • We have access to an electrical outlet (if needed) — we also have a generator.

Mobile sessions may be subject to travel fees beyond our standard radius.

Product ingredients & allergens

We use natural, plant‑based ingredients such as argan oil, shea butter, oat extract, chamomile, and aloe vera. While these are generally safe, we cannot guarantee that every dog will tolerate every ingredient. If your dog has known allergies, please inform us during booking. We can perform a patch test 24 hours before the session.

For severe allergies, we offer a hypoallergenic conditioning line that is fragrance‑free and uses minimal ingredients.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to book in advance?

Yes, we recommend booking at least 2–3 days in advance to secure your preferred time. Same‑day bookings may be available depending on our schedule — just give us a call!

What areas do you serve for mobile services?

We currently serve Austin and surrounding areas within a 15‑mile radius. Travel fees may apply for locations further out. Contact us for a custom quote.

Can I bring my dog for a consultation first?

Absolutely! We offer free 15‑minute consultations to assess your dog’s coat and recommend the best conditioning treatment. Just give us a call to schedule.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), cash, and mobile payments (Apple Pay, Google Pay). Payment is due at the time of service.

Visit Our Studio

Our studio is designed to be a calm, welcoming space for both you and your dog. We have a comfortable waiting area with refreshments, and our conditioning stations are equipped with the latest tools to ensure your pup feels relaxed and pampered.

When you arrive, you'll be greeted by one of our friendly squad members who will guide you through the process. We'll discuss your dog's coat type, any specific concerns, and tailor the treatment to their unique needs. We also offer complimentary paw balm and a spritz of our signature lavender‑chamomile mist after every session.

Parking & Accessibility

We have free parking available directly in front of our studio. The entrance is wheelchair‑accessible, and we have a ramp for easy access. If you require any special accommodations, please let us know in advance so we can ensure a smooth visit.

Mobile Services — We Come to You!

Can't make it to our studio? No problem! Our fully equipped mobile conditioning van brings the Boop Squad experience directly to your doorstep. We have everything needed for a complete conditioning session — from warm water tanks to high‑quality products. Our mobile service is perfect for busy pet parents, anxious pups, or anyone who prefers the convenience of at‑home care.

To book a mobile appointment, simply select "Mobile Service" in the subject dropdown of our contact form, or give us a call. We'll work with you to find a time that fits your schedule.

Partnerships & Community

Boop Squad is proud to partner with local shelters and rescue organisations. For every 10 conditioning sessions, we donate a full grooming kit to a rescue dog in need. We also host monthly "Coat Clinics" — free workshops where we demonstrate advanced brushing and conditioning techniques. Check our social media or sign up for our newsletter to stay updated on upcoming events.

Get Social With Us

Follow us on Instagram, Facebook, and TikTok to see our latest work, get tips on coat care, and meet the dogs we've conditioned. We love sharing before‑and‑after transformations, product recommendations, and behind‑the‑scenes content. Tag us in your pup's photos — we might feature you!

We can't wait to meet you and your furry friend. Whether you're a first‑time visitor or a loyal squad member, we're here to provide the best coat conditioning experience in Austin. Reach out today — we're just a call, click, or wag away.

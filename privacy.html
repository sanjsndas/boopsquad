Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026 — Boop Squad (“we”, “us”, “our”) values your trust and is committed to protecting the privacy of every pet parent, visitor, and furry friend. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and safeguard your personal information when you interact with our website, book services, or engage with us in any way.

your privacy is our priority

1. Information we collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our customers — both human and canine. The types of information we gather include:

  • Personal identification data: Name, email address, phone number, postal address, and pet details (name, breed, age, coat type, health notes).
  • Transaction data: Payment information, booking history, service preferences, and appointment records.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device identifiers, cookies, and usage patterns when you visit boopsquad.co.
  • Communication data: Records of your inquiries, feedback, survey responses, and any correspondence with our team.
  • Pet health & coat data: Allergies, skin sensitivities, previous grooming history, and specific conditioning needs — all stored securely to tailor our service.

How we collect information

We collect data directly from you when you:

  • Fill out our online booking form or contact form.
  • Subscribe to our newsletter or loyalty program.
  • Interact with us via email, phone, or social media.
  • Use our mobile conditioning service (on‑site data entry).
  • Accept cookies on our website (see our Cookies Policy).

We also receive data from trusted third‑party booking platforms (e.g., when you book through our partners) — always with your consent.

2. How we use your information

Boop Squad uses your personal data for the following purposes:

  • Service delivery: To process bookings, prepare custom conditioning blends, send appointment reminders, and follow up after each session.
  • Customer support: To respond to your questions, resolve issues, and provide proactive assistance.
  • Personalization: To remember your pet’s preferences, coat history, and favorite products, so we can offer a bespoke experience every time.
  • Marketing (with consent): To share promotions, seasonal offers, educational content, and new product launches. You can opt out anytime.
  • Improvement & analytics: To analyze usage trends, measure the effectiveness of our website, and enhance our services.
  • Legal compliance: To meet regulatory obligations, prevent fraud, and protect our rights or the safety of our customers.
We never sell your personal data to third parties. Your trust is the foundation of our squad.

3. Legal basis for processing

We process your data based on one or more of the following legal grounds:

  • Contractual necessity: To fulfill our service agreement (e.g., booking and delivering conditioning treatments).
  • Legitimate interests: To improve our services, communicate effectively, and maintain security — always balanced with your rights.
  • Consent: For marketing communications, cookies (non‑essential), and certain data sharing where you have given explicit permission.
  • Legal obligation: To comply with applicable laws, tax regulations, or court orders.

4. Sharing your information

We share your data only in specific, limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: Payment processors, booking platforms, email delivery services, and IT support — all bound by strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Professional advisors: Legal, accounting, or insurance professionals as needed.
  • Legal authorities: When required by law, to protect our rights, or to investigate fraud or safety concerns.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred — with continued protection under this policy.

We never share your data with advertisers or data brokers. Any third‑party processors are vetted for GDPR/CCPA compliance.

5. Data security

We implement robust security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption (SSL/TLS) for all data transmitted via our website.
  • Access controls — only authorised squad members have access to client records.
  • Regular security audits, vulnerability scans, and staff training on data protection.
  • Secure storage of physical records (paper forms) in locked facilities.
  • Anonymization and pseudonymization where possible.

While no system is 100% immune, we strive to maintain the highest standards of data integrity and confidentiality.

6. Your rights (GDPR, CCPA, and more)

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Erasure: Ask us to delete your data (subject to legal retention obligations).
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data.
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine‑readable format.
  • Objection: Opt out of marketing or certain processing activities.
  • Withdraw consent: Anytime, without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@boopsquad.co or call (512) 555‑BOOP. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Cookies & tracking technologies

We use cookies and similar tools to enhance your experience, analyse traffic, and personalise content. For detailed information, please see our Cookies Policy. You can manage your cookie preferences at any time via our cookie consent banner.

8. Data retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law. For example:

  • Booking records: retained for 7 years for tax and legal compliance.
  • Pet health notes: retained for 5 years after your last session to inform future care.
  • Marketing data: retained until you unsubscribe or request deletion.
  • Analytics data: anonymised after 26 months.

We periodically review our data inventory and securely delete or anonymise information that is no longer needed.

9. International data transfers

Boop Squad is based in the United States (Texas). If you are visiting from the EU, UK, or other regions, please note that your data may be transferred to and processed in the US. We ensure appropriate safeguards (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses) are in place for such transfers.

10. Children’s privacy

Our services are directed at pet owners who are at least 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we become aware of such data, we will delete it promptly.

11. Third‑party links

Our website may contain links to external sites (e.g., booking platforms, social media). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties. We encourage you to review their policies before providing any personal information.

12. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised. We will notify you of material changes via email or by posting a prominent notice on our website.

Our commitment to continuous improvement
